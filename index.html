About BABYMONSTER

BABYMONSTER is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment. Known as the "Monster Rookies," they have captivated audiences worldwide even before their official debut. With their exceptional vocal and dance abilities, BABYMONSTER represents the new wave of K-pop stars ready to make history.
